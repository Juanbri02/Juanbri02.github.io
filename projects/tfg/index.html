About the project

I developed this project under the joint supervision of professor Andrew Dancer at the Mathematical Institute of the University of Oxford, and Eva Miranda at the SYMCREA lab of the Polythecnic University of Catalonia during the 24/25 academic year. It covers topics on symplectic geometry, focusing on singular symplectic reduction.
